A Legacy of Quiet Luxury

For over a century, Velora has stood as a silent witness to the evolving language of elegance. Born in the artisanal heart of Florence, our house was founded on the principle that true luxury whispered rather than shouted.

Each piece is a testament to the patient hands of our craftsmen, who blend ancestral techniques with a modern architectural sensibility. We do not chase trends; we curate moments of permanence in an ephemeral world.
